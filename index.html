Why Choose Us

The primary reason why you should choose us is that we offer you a wide range of good quality vegetable, paddy and jute seeds at affordable price. We have earned our precious credibility in our journey through one hundred years.

With an eye on the various agricultural needs of our clients and to save them the hassle and toil of running about, we have set up the following five associate companies. National Laboratories produces micro nutrients, hormones, bio-fertilizers and bio-pesticides for the growth and well- being of plants and fish growth promoters. Bharat Fresh supplies Fresh Vegetables and Fresh and Dried Fruits. Ananda Agency pblishes books on agriculture. Jyoti Biotech is engaged in research and development work and also produces seeds. Our Research and Development Wing has been approved by the Department of Scientific and Industrial Research (DSIR), New Delhi. We are in close relationship with a number of reputed Indian and foreign companies. We have a strong network throughout India that ensures smooth and speedy delivery of goods to our customers.
